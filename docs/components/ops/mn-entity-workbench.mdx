# MnEntityWorkbench

Workbench view for inspecting and editing a single business entity.

## When to use

Use to provide a full editing surface for a complex domain entity.

| `activeTabId` | `string` | — | — |

| `onTabClose` | `(tabId: string) =&gt; void` | — | — |
| `onTabAdd` | `() =&gt; void` | — | — |
| `onSave` | `(tabId: string) =&gt; void` | — | — |
| `onReorder` | `(tabIds: string[]) =&gt; void` | — | — |

| `renderContent` | `(tab: EntityWorkbenchTab) =&gt; React.ReactNode` | — | — |
